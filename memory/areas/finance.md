# Finance Admin

## Context

- Assume UK tax residency.
- User wants the assistant to flag financial opportunities and recommend practical next steps.

## Areas Of Interest

- Investment and crypto review.
- Bills and subscriptions.
- Tax-efficiency opportunities.
- Document and note organization.

## Caution

Recommendations should be clear about when qualified UK tax, legal, or regulated financial advice is appropriate.

## Open Questions

- Where finance documents currently live.
- Which accounts, wallets, providers, and subscriptions should be tracked.
- Preferred review cadence.
